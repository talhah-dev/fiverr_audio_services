Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about booking, on-site recording, and delivery.

To book a service, simply submit a request with your event date, location, and the type of service you require. You can select one of our packages and indicate any optional add-ons. We will provide a tailored quote and confirm availability by email, ensuring the process is clear and straightforward from the start.

No online payments are processed through our website. Once your booking is confirmed, we issue an invoice and arrange payment through a secure payment link, giving you flexibility and security.

To help us provide the most accurate quote and ensure smooth planning, please include your event date, location, type of service required, preferred package, and any add-ons. Additional details such as expected number of participants, venue size, or technical requirements are also helpful.

We offer services across London and UK. Whether your event is local or national, our team can provide support wherever your production takes place.

Delivered audio is fully production-ready, in formats suited to your workflow and post-production needs. Standard delivery timelines are agreed during booking, with options for expedited turnaround where required.
